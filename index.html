Transforming the Marketing industry with A.I

In the world of Science and Technology today, the use of A.I has become very prevalent. Almost every industry/organisation have employed the use of A.I in their day-to-day operations. Technology gaints such as Apple and Google have made it so that A.I is avaliable to everyone irregardless of their location, socio-economic class, and educational level. Since it,s inception, many industries have benefited from the use of A.I in their planning and strategizing. However, there have been discussions on the downside of this technological miracle. This project aims to highlight the positive effects on A.I in the marketing sector and it's effectiveness on achieving the following:

Consumer behaviour

A.I will be able to monitor consumer behaviour at all times and accurately predict market trends globally

Personalization

A.I would be able to assist marketers in personalizing marketing strategies according to the needs of their target audience.

Accuracy

Marketers are rest assured that every documentation carried out from the beginning of customers journeys, to the end are accurate.
