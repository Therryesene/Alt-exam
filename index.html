The Future of A.I in marketing.

Artifical Intelligence in Marketing

In the world of Science and Technology today, the use of A.I has become very prevalent. Almost every industry/organisation have employed the use of A.I in their day-to-day operations. Technology gaints such as Apple and Google have made it so that A.I is avaliable to everyone irregardless of their location, socio-economic class, and educational level. Since it,s inception, Many industries have benefited from the use of A.I in their planning and strategizing. However, there have been discussions on the downside of this technological miracle.

This project aims to not only highlight the positive effects on A.I in the marketing sector but also shed light on how this invention may affect consumer behaviour, data protection, and consumers' purchasing intent and power. As the lead researcher on this project, my scope of study will be limited to Lagos, Nigeria and I will be employing the use of interviews and questionnaires to gather data. Results gotten from this research will assist marketers in employing the use of A.I in an ethical manner when creating marketing strategies.
